Who is Elise you might ask? Well, Elise is a singer/songwriter, producer, dancer, actress and director.
She's a leader, teacher, and a therapist. Whatever you ask her to do she can do it. Many people want her to be many things. A friendly acquaintance, friend, close friend, best friend, sister, mother figure, or even an enemey.

From the likes of Ella Fitzgeral all the way to Beyonce. You name it, she likes it. So why don't we dive deeper into the wonderful world of music with Elise.
